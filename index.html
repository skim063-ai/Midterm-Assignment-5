Welcome

I would like to send the warmest welcome to everyone that shares the passion of basketball. Based out of Northern California our mission statement is to pursue pure excellence in all we do. This page is dedicated to developing aspiring basketball players of all ages, backgrounds, and walks of life. It doesn't matter whether you laced up your shoes for the first time or have played many seasons as professional. The Nor-Cal community stands behind the pursuit to challenge and get continue to improve your basketball abilities.
